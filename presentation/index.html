What is blockchain?

What is blockchain?

Blockchain is a chain of blocks with information

Each block has transaction data

Each block has its own unique hash

The block cannot be modified

When was it created?

  • 1982 - David Chaum described the technology
  • 1991 - Mathematicians S. Haber and W. Scott Stornetta developed a system
  • 2009 - Satoshi Nakamoto created Bitcoin
  • 2015 - Vitalik Buterin created Ethereum

What Can Blockchain Technology do?

The network can connect to users and make direct transactions between them

Blockchain ensures secure transactions

Digital currency is based on blockchain technology (Bitcoin,Ethereum)

They can be created, transferred, and stored outside the control of any government

Advantages

  • Improved accuracy by removing human involvement in verification
  • Cost reductions by eliminating third-party verification
  • Immutability of recorded data
  • Decentralization makes it harder to tamper with
  • Transactions are secure, private, and efficient
  • Transparency of data

Disadvantages

  • Low transactions per second
  • Use in illicit activities(dark web)
  • Regulation remains uncertain
  • Data storage limitations
